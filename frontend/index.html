    Nature vs. Fiction

    Tap a card to reveal the green truth.

    Myth

    "Plastic is fully recyclable"

    Reveal Truth

    Fact

    Only ~9% of plastic is recycled. Reduce & Reuse is the real solution!

    Myth

    "My actions are too small"

    Reveal Truth

    Fact

    Collective action drives policy. 1 million people making small changes creates huge impact.

    Myth

    "Paper bags are eco-friendly"

    Reveal Truth

    Fact

    Paper production uses huge amounts of water/energy. Reusable bags are best!

    Myth

    "Climate change is natural"

    Reveal Truth

    Fact

    Current warming is 10x faster than natural cycles, driven by human emissions.

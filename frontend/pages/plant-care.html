Gardening Tips & Tricks

Sunlight

Understand your plant's light needs. Most vegetables need 6-8 hours of direct sun, while many indoor plants prefer indirect light.

Watering

Overwatering is the #1 killer of plants. Always check if the top inch of soil is dry before watering again.

Composting

Turn kitchen scraps into gold. Use fruit peels, coffee grounds, and eggshells to create nutrient-rich soil.

Plant Guide

Indoor vs Outdoor Plants

Indoor Plants

  • Snake Plant: Purifies air, low light.
  • Peace Lily: Beautiful blooms, needs shade.
  • Spider Plant: Easy to grow and propagate.
  • Pothos: Trailing vine, very hardy.

Outdoor Plants

  • Marigolds: Natural pest repellent.
  • Basil & Herbs: Need warmth and sun.
  • Hibiscus: Stunning tropical flowers.
  • Aloe Vera: Medicinal and drought tolerant.

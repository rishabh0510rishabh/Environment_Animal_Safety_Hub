Recycle Properly

Learn what can and cannot be recycled in your area. Clean and sort materials correctly to ensure they get a second life.

Compost Organics

Turn food scraps and yard waste into nutrient-rich compost for your garden instead of sending it to landfill.

Refuse Single-Use

Say no to disposable items like plastic bags, straws, and packaging. Choose reusable alternatives instead.

Reuse Creatively

Find new purposes for items before discarding them. Repurpose containers, repair items, and donate what you no longer need.

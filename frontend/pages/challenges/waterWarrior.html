Why Water Conservation Matters

Global Water Crisis

2.2 billion people lack access to safely managed drinking water. By 2025, half the world's population will be living in water-stressed areas.

Household Water Use

The average American family uses more than 300 gallons of water per day at home. 70% of this is used indoors, primarily in bathrooms.

Virtual Water

It takes 1,847 gallons of water to produce 1 pound of beef. A single almond requires 1.1 gallons of water to grow.

Energy Connection

8% of global energy generation is used for pumping, treating, and transporting water. Saving water saves energy too!

Easy Water-Saving Tips

Small changes can add up to big savings. Try these simple strategies during your Water Warrior Week:

Shorter Showers

Cut your shower time by just 2 minutes to save up to 10 gallons of water per shower.

Turn Off Tap

Don't let the water run while brushing teeth or washing hands. Save 4 gallons per minute.

Full Loads Only

Run dishwashers and washing machines only when full. Save 15-45 gallons per load.

Reuse Water

Collect shower warm-up water for plants. Reuse pasta water for garden watering.

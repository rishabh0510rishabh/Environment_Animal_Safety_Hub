Take the Challenge

Welcome to the Plastic-Free Week Challenge! For the next 7 days, you'll avoid all single-use plastics. This means saying no to plastic bags, bottles, straws, cutlery, packaging, and any other disposable plastic items. Instead, you'll use reusable alternatives that are better for our planet.

This beginner-friendly challenge will help you develop sustainable habits that reduce plastic pollution, conserve resources, and lower your carbon footprint. You'll join over 1,200 participants who are making a difference one day at a time!

Reusable Bags

Keep reusable bags in your car, backpack, or by your door so you never forget them when shopping.

Water Bottles

Carry a refillable water bottle and coffee cup to avoid disposable containers.

Food Containers

Bring your own containers for takeout and leftovers to avoid plastic packaging.

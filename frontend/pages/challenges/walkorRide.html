Take the 7-Day Active Transportation Challenge

For the next 7 days, you'll replace car trips with walking, biking, or other active transportation methods. Whether it's your commute to work, a trip to the grocery store, or visiting friends, find ways to leave the car behind.

This intermediate-level challenge helps you build healthier habits, reduce your carbon footprint, save money on gas, and discover your community from a new perspective. Join nearly 900 participants who are making their journeys more sustainable!

Health Benefits

Improve cardiovascular health, boost mood, increase energy levels, and maintain a healthy weight.

Environmental Impact

Reduce air pollution, decrease carbon emissions, and lower your ecological footprint.

Financial Savings

Save on gas, parking fees, car maintenance, and potentially reduce insurance costs.

Getting Started Tips

Make your active transportation week successful with these practical tips:

Plan Your Routes

Use apps like Google Maps or Strava to find bike-friendly routes and walking paths.

Bike Maintenance

Ensure your bike is in good condition: check tires, brakes, and chain before starting.

Weather Preparation

Check the forecast and have rain gear, sunscreen, or layers ready for changing conditions.

Trip Combination

Combine errands into one trip and use a backpack or bike baskets to carry items.

Time Management

Allow extra time for your journeys, especially when first transitioning from driving.

Stay Hydrated

Bring a water bottle, especially for longer bike rides or walks in warm weather.

Safety Tips

Stay safe while walking or biking:

Visibility

Wear bright or reflective clothing, especially at dawn, dusk, or night.

Helmet Safety

Always wear a properly fitted helmet when biking.

Follow Traffic Rules

Obey traffic signals, use bike lanes where available, and signal your turns.

Be Predictable

Ride in a straight line, don't weave between parked cars.

Stay Alert

Avoid distractions like phones or headphones in traffic.

Lock Your Bike

Use a sturdy U-lock and secure both frame and wheels.

Why Digital Detox Matters

Energy Savings

Powering down devices for a weekend reduces electricity consumption significantly. One smartphone charger left plugged in uses ~0.26W constantly.

E-Waste Awareness

Reducing screen time extends device lifespan, delaying when they become e-waste. The world generates ~50 million tons of e-waste annually.

Mental Clarity

Constant notifications fragment attention. A digital break can improve focus, reduce anxiety, and enhance creativity.

Better Sleep

Blue light from screens disrupts melatonin production. Unplugging before bed improves sleep quality and overall health.

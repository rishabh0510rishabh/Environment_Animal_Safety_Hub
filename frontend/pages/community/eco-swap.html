Safety Guide

Meet in Public Places

Always meet in well-lit, public locations like cafes, shopping malls, or community centers.

Bring a Friend

Never meet alone. Bring a friend or family member with you for added security.

Daytime Meetings

Schedule exchanges during daylight hours when possible.

Share Your Plans

Let someone know where you're going, who you're meeting, and when you'll be back.

Inspect Items Carefully

Check items thoroughly before completing the exchange. Don't feel pressured.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, cancel the meeting. Your safety comes first.

Avoid Sharing Personal Info

Don't share your home address, financial details, or sensitive personal information.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you encounter suspicious behavior, report it to our team immediately.

How to Get Started

Don't feel overwhelmed! Follow these simple steps to begin your zero-waste journey.

1

Audit Your Waste

Look at what you're throwing away for one week. Identify the biggest sources of waste in your home.

2

Start with One Room

Begin with the kitchen or bathroom. Master one area before moving to the next.

3

Use What You Have

Don't buy new things immediately. First, use up what you already own.

4

Add One Swap at a Time

Introduce new habits gradually. Master one swap before adding another.

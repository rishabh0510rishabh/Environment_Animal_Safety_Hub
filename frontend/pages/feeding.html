Recommended Diet

Safety Guidelines

Do Feed

Natural grains, fresh fruits, vegetables, and species-specific commercial food.

Don't Feed

Chocolate, salty snacks, bread (bad for birds), onions, or spicy human food.

Hydration

Clean water is vital. Ensure water bowls are washed and refilled twice daily.

üåä Top Conservation Strategies

Practical tips to reduce your water footprint

1

Shorter Showers

Reduce shower time by 2 minutes to save ~20 L per shower

üíß Saves ~7,300 L/year
2

Fix Leaks

A dripping tap wastes up to 15 L per day. Fix it immediately!

üíß Saves ~5,500 L/year
3

Full Loads Only

Run dishwasher and washing machine only when full

üíß Saves ~30 L per load
4

Plant-Based Diet

Reduce meat consumption, especially beef, for huge water savings

üíß Saves ~15,000 L/kg beef
5

Install Aerators

Low-flow faucet aerators reduce water flow by 30-50%

üíß Saves ~11,000 L/year
6

Dual-Flush Toilets

Use half-flush option when possible

üíß Saves ~6-9 L per flush
7

Smart Lawn Care

Water lawn early morning, use drip irrigation, collect rainwater

üíß Saves ~40% outdoor usage
8

Buy Secondhand

Buying used clothing saves all the water from production

üíß Saves ~2,700 L per shirt

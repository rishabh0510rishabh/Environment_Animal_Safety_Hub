Safety First

Safety Tips

Important guidelines to ensure safe reunions and prevent scams.

Verify Identity

Always ask for proof of ownership (photos, vet records, microchip info) before handing over a pet.

Meet in Public

Arrange meetings in public places like parks or vet clinics. Never share your home address initially.

Use Our Messaging

Communicate through our secure messaging system. Don't share personal contact details immediately.

Report Suspicious Activity

If something feels wrong, report it to our team immediately. We verify all listings.

How to Protect Yourself

Choose Smart Seafood

Eat smaller fish (sardines, salmon) instead of large predators (shark, swordfish)

Go Organic

Reduce pesticide intake by choosing organic produce when possible

Proper Disposal

Recycle electronics properly to prevent heavy metal contamination

Stay Informed

Check local fish advisories for mercury and PCB warnings

Make a Difference Today

Simple choices in what you eat and buy can significantly reduce your exposure to biomagnified toxins.

What to Do While Waiting for Help

  • Keep your distance - Do not approach or touch the animal
  • Keep pets and children away - Maintain a safe perimeter
  • Do not feed or give water - This can cause more harm
  • Observe from afar - Monitor the animal's condition
  • Stay available - Keep your phone on for rescue team contact
  • Document if safe - Take photos from a distance if possible

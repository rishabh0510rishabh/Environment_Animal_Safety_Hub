Safe Feeding Guide

üêï
Dogs - Safe Foods
Dry dog food, cooked rice, boiled chicken (no bones), plain bread. Avoid chocolate, onions, grapes, and spicy foods.
üê±
Cats - Safe Foods
Dry cat food, cooked fish, boiled chicken, cat treats. Never give milk, tuna in oil, or raw fish.
üíß
Fresh Water
Always provide clean, fresh water in shallow bowls. Change water daily and clean bowls regularly.
‚è∞
Feeding Schedule
Feed at consistent times daily. Morning and evening are best. Don't overfeed - small, regular meals are better.
üßº
Hygiene
Use separate bowls for each animal. Clean feeding area regularly. Wash hands before and after feeding.

Quick Tips

  • Approach stray animals slowly and calmly
  • Never force interaction - let them come to you
  • Keep feeding areas clean and organized
  • Take photos to help identify lost pets
  • Contact local shelters before taking animals
  • Spay/neuter prevents overpopulation

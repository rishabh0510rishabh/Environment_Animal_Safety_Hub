The Trojan Hives: Disease Transmission from Managed to Wild Bees

Pathogen spillover from commercial beekeeping represents a significant threat to wild pollinator populations. Diseases amplified in managed honeybee colonies can spread to native bees, butterflies, and other pollinators through shared floral resources and environmental contamination.

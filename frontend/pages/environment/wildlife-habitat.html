Legal Protections

International and national laws safeguarding wildlife habitats

Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD)

International treaty aimed at conserving biodiversity, sustainable use of resources, and fair sharing of benefits.

CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species)

Regulates international trade in wildlife and plants to prevent over-exploitation of species.

National Park Laws

Country-specific legislation establishing protected areas and regulating activities within them.

Endangered Species Acts

Laws protecting threatened and endangered species and their habitats from harm.

Water Conservation Tips

Smart ways to reduce your water footprint

Shower & Bath

  • Take 5-minute showers instead of baths
  • Install low-flow showerheads (6 L/min)
  • Turn off water while soaping
  • Collect cold water while waiting for hot
Save up to: 50 L/day

Toilet

  • Install dual-flush toilets (3/6 L)
  • Don't use toilet as trash bin
  • Fix leaks immediately
  • Use half-flush when possible
Save up to: 30 L/day

Kitchen

  • Run dishwasher only when full
  • Use basin for hand washing dishes
  • Keep drinking water in fridge
  • Thaw food in fridge, not under tap
Save up to: 40 L/day

Laundry

  • Only wash full loads
  • Use water-efficient machines
  • Reuse greywater for garden
  • Choose cold water cycles
Save up to: 100 L/week

Garden & Outdoor

  • Water early morning or evening
  • Use drip irrigation systems
  • Collect rainwater in barrels
  • Choose drought-resistant plants
Save up to: 200 L/week

General Tips

  • Fix all leaks promptly
  • Install aerators on taps
  • Turn off taps when not needed
  • Monitor your water meter
Save up to: 80 L/day

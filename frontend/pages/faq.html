Animal Care & Safety

What should I do if I find an injured animal?

First, ensure your safety. Contact local wildlife authorities or veterinarians immediately. Do not attempt to treat the animal yourself unless you have proper training. Keep the animal calm and in a quiet, safe place until help arrives.

How can I safely feed stray animals?

Provide clean water and appropriate food. For dogs: dry kibble or cooked rice with vegetables. For cats: cat food or cooked fish. Avoid chocolate, onions, grapes, and spicy foods. Always maintain distance and let the animal approach you.

When should I report animal abuse?

Report immediately if you witness physical harm, neglect, abandonment, or unsafe living conditions. Use our report form or contact local animal control authorities. Document evidence safely if possible.

Environmental Protection

How do I properly segregate waste?

Separate waste into categories:

  • Recyclable: Paper, plastic bottles, glass, metal cans
  • Organic: Food scraps, garden waste
  • Hazardous: Batteries, electronics, chemicals
  • General: Non-recyclable items

What are simple ways to reduce my carbon footprint?

Start with: using public transport, reducing energy consumption, eating less meat, buying local products, using reusable bags, conserving water, and planting trees. Small changes make a big difference!

How can I report environmental issues?

Use our report form to document pollution, illegal dumping, or environmental damage. Include photos, location details, and time of incident. We'll forward reports to appropriate authorities.

Plant Care & Gardening

What are the best plants for beginners?

Start with low-maintenance plants like pothos, snake plants, aloe vera, mint, basil, or marigolds. These require minimal care and are forgiving to beginners.

How often should I water my plants?

Check soil moisture by inserting your finger 1-2 inches deep. Water when soil feels dry. Most indoor plants need watering 1-2 times per week, while outdoor plants may need daily watering in hot weather.

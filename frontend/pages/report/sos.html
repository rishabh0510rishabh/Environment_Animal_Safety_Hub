When to Use Animal SOS

Use Animal SOS when animals are injured, trapped, abandoned, or in immediate danger.

Injured Animal

Use SOS when an animal is hurt due to accidents or attacks.

Trapped Animal

Report animals stuck in drains, fences, or unsafe places.

Baby Animal Alone

If a baby animal is abandoned or separated from its mother.

Abuse or Danger

When an animal is being harmed or in immediate danger.

Safety Instructions

Follow these instructions to ensure your safety and avoid further harm to animals.

Stay Calm

Keep distance and avoid sudden movements.

Offer Water

Only if the animal is conscious and safe to approach.

Do Not Move

Never move injured animals without expert help.

Wait for Help

Allow trained rescuers to handle the situation.

Emergency Safety Tips

Follow these safety tips carefully while waiting for rescue support.

Stay Calm

Do not panic and stay calm to avoid frightening the animal.

Avoid Crowding

Keep people away to reduce stress and prevent further injury.

Offer Water

Give water only if the animal is conscious and safe to approach.

Call Helpline

Immediately contact the helpline if the situation is critical.

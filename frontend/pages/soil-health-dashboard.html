Tips for Improving Soil Health

  • Rotate crops to prevent nutrient depletion.
  • Add compost or organic matter regularly.
  • Test soil annually for pH and nutrients.
  • Use cover crops to protect and enrich soil.
  • Avoid over-tilling to maintain soil structure.

About Extinction

Species extinction is a natural process accelerated by human activities. Habitat loss, overhunting, climate change, and pollution have led to the disappearance of countless species. Understanding extinction helps us protect the remaining biodiversity on Earth.

Major Causes of Extinction

Habitat Loss

Destruction of natural habitats through deforestation, urbanization, and agriculture.

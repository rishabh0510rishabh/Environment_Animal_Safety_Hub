Why Animal First Aid Matters

Animals often face injuries, accidents, or health emergencies. Knowing basic first-aid can help stabilize them until professional veterinary care is available.

Basic First-Aid Steps

  • Stay calm and ensure your safety first
  • Check for breathing and visible injuries
  • Stop bleeding using clean cloth or bandage
  • Keep the animal warm and comfortable

Emergency Care Tips

  • Move injured animals carefully
  • Avoid giving human medicines
  • Contact a nearby veterinarian or animal rescue
  • Transport the animal safely if needed

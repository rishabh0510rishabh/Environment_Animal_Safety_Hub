Wet Waste

Includes food scraps, vegetable peels, fruit waste, tea leaves, and biodegradable items. Best composted to create natural manure.

Dry Waste

Includes plastic, paper, cardboard, glass, and metal. Should be clean and dry before recycling.

Hazardous Waste

Includes batteries, medicines, chemicals, sanitary waste, and e-waste. Requires special disposal methods.

  • Rinse containers before recycling
  • Never mix hazardous waste with regular waste

  • Teach children the importance of segregation

Safe Food Guidelines

  • Provide clean, fresh water at all times - change daily
  • Feed fresh, unseasoned, cooked food only
  • Use separate clean bowls for food and water
  • Feed at consistent times and safe locations
  • Store animal food properly to prevent contamination
  • Wash feeding bowls regularly with safe disinfectants

Animal Health Tips

  • Observe animals for signs of illness or injury
  • Keep feeding areas clean to prevent disease spread
  • Provide shelter along with food in extreme weather
  • Learn about local wildlife feeding requirements
  • Consult veterinarians for dietary recommendations
  • Monitor animal behavior for any unusual changes

Do's

  • Feed dogs plain rice, roti, or boiled vegetables
  • Offer safe fruits like apples, bananas, watermelon
  • Be calm and gentle while approaching animals
  • Feed in quiet areas away from traffic
  • Wash hands before and after handling animal food
  • Respect animal's space and feeding boundaries

Don'ts

  • Never give spicy, salty, or oily human food
  • Avoid chocolate, onions, grapes, and junk food
  • Don't tease or scare animals while feeding
  • Avoid feeding near busy roads or crowded areas
  • Don't force animals to eat if they're not hungry
  • Never leave plastic or harmful materials behind
